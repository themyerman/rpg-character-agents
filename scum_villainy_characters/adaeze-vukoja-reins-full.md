### Backstory
She was born in the maintenance crawlspaces of a Hegemonic supply hauler and enlisted at sixteen because the Marines were the only people who'd feed her three meals a day. On the boarding action at Khepri-7 something in the derelict's reactor *spoke* to her, and afterward she could not stop hearing the dead crew arguing in the walls — a fact her commanding officer rewarded with a medical discharge and a sealed file. Now she sells her ear to a smuggling crew, telling herself she's just listening for trouble, but every job pulls her closer to whatever the Ur left whispering in her skull.
