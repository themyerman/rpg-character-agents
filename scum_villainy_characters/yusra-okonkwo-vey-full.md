### Backstory
Yusra cut her teeth running protection for the Vey syndicate in the asteroid warrens of Tannhauser Reach, then sold her gun to whoever could meet the price — Hegemony-aligned barons mostly, the irony of which she tries not to examine. Six months ago a hydroponics engineer named Theo bandaged a knife wound she should have died from and asked nothing in return, and now she counts the days until a job goes wrong and her past catches up to the only good thing in her life. She signed on with this crew because the pay's enough to buy her way clean — one more score, she keeps telling herself, and she'll never have to lie to Theo about where the money came from.
