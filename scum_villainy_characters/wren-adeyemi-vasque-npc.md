## **Wren Adeyemi-Vasque**
*Freelance void-pilot — flies like she's trying to find the edge of something, and might not pull up when she does.*

| | |
|---|---|
| **Playbook** | Pilot |
| **Heritage** | Ur |
| **Background** | Nobility |
| **Vice** | Luxury / a private booth at the Cygnet Room, where the sommelier knows her by sight |

**Key Actions:** Helm (d8) — she threads atmospheric tears and debris fields by feel, eyes half-closed. Attune (d8) — Ur-touched, she hears the ship's resonance like a second pulse, knows trouble three seconds before the instruments do. Consort (d8) — old finishing-school manners she can't quite scrub off.

**Demeanor:** Immaculate posture, a coat two seasons out of fashion but tailored to the centimeter, and the careful slowness of someone managing a tremor in her left hand. She listens more than she speaks, and when she does speak it's dry, precise, and a little late.
**Wants:** A clean six-hour sleep without the dreams, and enough creds to settle her tab at the Cygnet before the manager stops being polite about it.
**Secret:** She was the only survivor of the *Halberd Lyssa* incident — eighty-four crew lost when she made the call to vent the foredecks. The official record says equipment failure. She knows the records were edited because her family paid to edit them.
**Hook:** She'll fly a job for half the going rate if the crew can get her into a specific Hegemonic archive vault on Iri Xochi — she wants the unedited flight recorder, and she won't say whether she wants to destroy it or listen to it.
**Connection:** Her younger brother **Téo Adeyemi-Vasque**, a rising diplomat on Procyon who has not answered her messages in two years. She sends him a letter every month anyway. She does not keep copies.
