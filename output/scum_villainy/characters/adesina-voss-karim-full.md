### Backstory
Adesina was born on a long-haul ore freighter that changed registries six times before she turned twelve, and she joined the Hegemonic Marines mostly because their recruiter promised an address. Three tours in, she was assigned as a "civil liaison" — which meant convincing border villages on Ulmus IV to surrender peacefully before the orbital guns spoke; one village said no, and she still hears the children's names she had memorized to build trust. She walked off the transport at her decommission and never reported in, drifting until a crew on the rim needed someone who could lie to a customs officer's face — that wasn't the plan, but neither was anything else.
