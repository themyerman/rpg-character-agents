## **Oziel Vantreaux-Mourran-Kepf**
*Half-Elf Failed Thief & Tavern Bard — A man whose smile has signed three marriage contracts and outrun four bounty hunters.*

| | |
|---|---|
| **CHA** | 16 (+3) |
| **DEX** | 16 (+3) |
| **WIS** | 11 (+0) |

**Demeanor:** Soft-spoken, always slightly rumpled, with ink-stained fingers and a lute slung backward across his shoulder like he forgot it was there. He listens too well, leans in too close, and remembers the name of your dead dog from a conversation six months ago.

**Wants:** Forty gold by next market-day to pay off a midwife in Vellsbruck who delivered his seventh child — she's threatening to write his wife in Hannox.

**Secret:** He genuinely loves all four wives. He keeps a locket with miniature portraits of every child (eleven, as of last spring) and weeps over it when drunk. The "failed thief" part isn't false modesty — he botched a jewel heist in Caerith two years ago and the guild marked his left earlobe; he keeps it covered with a leather cord and claims it's a bardic affectation.

**Hook:** He'll offer to guide the party to a "shortcut" through the hills — actually a detour past one of his wives' villages, where he needs to retrieve a hidden stash of letters before her brother finds them. He'll pay in songs, information, and one very nice (stolen) signet ring.

**Connection:** His eldest son, **Pell**, age twelve, in the fishing town of Sorrowmouth. The boy knows what his father is and writes him cold, formal letters that Oziel reads a hundred times each. Pell asked him last winter to please just stop coming back. Oziel hasn't answered that letter yet.
