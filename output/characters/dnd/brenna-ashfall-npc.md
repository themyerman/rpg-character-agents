## **Brenna Ashfall**
*Half-Orc Barbarian — A widow who walks into every fight first, smiling, hoping this one finally wins.*

| | |
|---|---|
| **STR** | 14 (+2) |
| **CON** | 12 (+1) |
| **WIS** | 11 (+0) |
| **CHA** | 10 (+0) |

**Demeanor:** Calm in a way that unsettles people — soft voice, steady hands, no flinch. She laughs at threats and offers strangers the last seat by the fire. The scars on her forearms are deliberate and recent.
**Wants:** A worthy death. Something she can call meaningful when the dark closes in — a dragon, a warlord, a doomed last stand. Anything but old age in a quiet bed.
**Secret:** Her three children died of redspot fever while she was off raiding for coin to pay the healer. The coin arrived two days late. She has the healer's unspent silver in a pouch around her neck and has never once opened it.
**Hook:** She'll volunteer — eagerly, cheerfully — for the suicide mission the party is trying to talk someone else into. She doesn't haggle. She doesn't ask what's on the other side. She just wants to know when they ride out.
**Connection:** Her younger brother **Korv**, a blacksmith in Hallow's Reach, still writes her letters she never answers. He thinks she's traveling to heal. He's saving a room for her above his forge.
