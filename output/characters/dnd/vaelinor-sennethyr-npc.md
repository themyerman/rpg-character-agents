## **Vaelinor Sennethyr**
*Human Hermit-Exile — A barefoot woman who walked out of a village eighty years ago and has since forgotten how to talk to the people who live in them.*

| | |
|---|---|
| **DEX** | 13 (+1) |
| **WIS** | 14 (+2) |
| **CHA** | 11 (+0) |

**Demeanor:** She stands too still and stares too long — the patience of something that has watched seasons rot. When she finally speaks, her Common is hesitant and oddly formal, like she's translating from a language she once knew well and has since let go fallow.
**Wants:** A particular kind of pale mushroom that only grows on the north face of a barrow three days east — she needs it before the next new moon, and she will not say why.
**Secret:** She wasn't exiled. She fled. Eighty years ago she was apprenticed to a hedge-witch who bargained with something from the deep woods — and when the bargain went wrong, Vaelinor ran rather than honoring the debt that was owed. The thing she owes is still waiting, and it has been patient. The mushroom is the first ingredient in a ritual she hopes will let her finally settle the account before it comes to collect.
**Hook:** She offers to guide the party through old-growth forest no map shows — in exchange, they help her retrieve "a thing left behind" from a barrow she refuses to enter herself.
**Connection:** **Dara Veth**, a traveling herbalist who passes through the region three times a year and always leaves a parcel at the base of a certain oak without ever knocking. She's the closest thing Vaelinor has to a friend, and the only person who knows what the mushroom is actually for. Dara thinks Vaelinor is going to get herself killed, and she's right.
