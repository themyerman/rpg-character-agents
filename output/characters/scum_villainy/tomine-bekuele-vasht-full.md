### Backstory
Tomine wore the grey of the 17th Hegemonic Marine Auxiliary for eleven years and rose to subaltern before the incident at Khaspur Relay — what actually happened there is locked behind a neural redaction she paid a back-alley stitch to crack, only to find the files corrupted and her left hand permanently tremoring whenever she tries to remember. The judges stripped her rank, her pension, and the right to wear her grandmother's prayer-knife in public, then dropped her on Indri with a one-way chit. She took a crew job because Vesh Naro's pit boss said she could clear her debt in six fights, and after the third she realized the only thing she still trusted was a loaded weapon and the people standing behind her.
