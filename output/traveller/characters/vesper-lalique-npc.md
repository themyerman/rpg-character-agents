## **Vesper Lalique**
*Entertainer (Famous) — A torch singer whose voice can stop a room and whose smile has ended at least two marriages.*

| | |
|---|---|
| **UPP** | 974BAC |
| **Career** | Entertainer — Famous Entertainer |
| **Notable Skills** | Persuade 3, Carouse 2, Deception 2 |

**Demeanor:** Moves like she knows you're watching, because you are. Lacquered black hair, a voice like warm bourbon, and a habit of touching your wrist when she laughs — as if every conversation is a duet she's letting you in on.
**Wants:** A discreet passage off Rhylanor within forty-eight hours, no questions on the manifest, no port authority scans.
**Secret:** Her last lover — a Sword Worlds attaché — left a data wafer in her dressing room before he was found dead. She hasn't opened it. She knows opening it ends her.
**Hook:** She approaches the most observant-looking character mid-set, slips them a private suite key with a folded note: *"I need someone who won't ask why. I can pay in credits or favors. Your choice."*
**Connection:** Her accompanist and oldest friend, **Doro Kev**, a blind pianist who raised her out of the Startown gutters. She won't leave the system without him — and he's currently being "questioned" by men in unmarked grey suits.
