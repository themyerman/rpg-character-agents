## **Suh-Yin Adomako**
*Merchant 3rd Officer — A wiry cargo handler with a wrenched-up shoulder and a ledger she won't let anyone read.*

| | |
|---|---|
| **UPP** | A49855 |
| **Career** | Merchant, 3rd Officer |
| **Notable Skills** | Broker-2, Mechanic-1, Streetwise-1 |

**Demeanor:** Talks fast, listens faster, and rolls her right shoulder constantly — an old loading-bay injury she refuses to get fixed because the insurance payout still comes monthly. Wears a battered crew jacket with the patches torn off.
**Wants:** A buyer for 14 crates of "agricultural enzyme" she received instead of the medical supplies she actually paid for. Three weeks before they spoil.
**Secret:** She knows the crates aren't enzyme. They're a precursor for a recreational drug banned in six subsectors. She figured it out on day two and has been pretending otherwise since.
**Hook:** Approaches the party in a dockside noodle bar offering 8% over standard freight rates for a "no-questions" run to a low-law world — and a finder's fee if they know a chemist.
**Connection:** Her former captain, **Berhane Okoro**, who fired her after the bad deal and still has her personal effects locked in his ship's hold — including a data wafer with her mother's last recorded message. She'd burn a lot of bridges to get it back.
