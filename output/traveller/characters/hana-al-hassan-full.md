### Career History

**Term 1 (age 18–22):** Hana signed onto the *free trader* circuit straight out of Qarn Reach — a noble girl from a rock where nobility meant nothing, eager to learn what the title might be worth elsewhere. She found her footing fast, brokered an unlikely deal for medical-grade cryogenics between two stations who'd refused to speak to each other for a decade, and earned the quiet respect of a senior broker named Jun Watanabe. Promoted from Crewman to Senior Crewman by her second year out.

**Term 2 (age 22–26):** A daring trade run — Hana convinced her captain to gamble on a load of contraband relics across a war-disputed border. The cargo paid out fivefold. But the captain she'd undercut to win the contract, Ruairí Flanagan, took the loss as personal and swore he'd ruin her. She made 4th Officer that year, with Flanagan's name in her ledger and his face in her memory.

**Term 3 (age 26–30):** A close call on the approach to Phelan's Run — a pirate sloop bracketed their ship and she talked the boarding party out of taking the bridge by convincing them the cargo was worth more whole than scattered. They left with a third of the manifest and her left thumbnail, taken as proof of compliance. She made 3rd Officer that same year. The thumbnail grew back crooked.

**Term 4 (age 30–34):** It was Flanagan after all. He'd patiently cultivated a junior crewmate — Hunahpu Tanaka, a quiet engineering hand Hana had trusted with her access codes — and when the second pirate boarding came it was no pirate at all but Flanagan's people walking in through unlocked airlocks. Tanaka vanished with the safe. The ship was stripped, the company folded, and Hana walked away with a small payout, a black mark, and a question about who else had known. Her merchant career ended at a customs office on Drennan, signing forms with a borrowed pen.

### Connections
- **Ally — Jun Watanabe:** A senior broker on the inner-trade circuit who mentored Hana through her first contract and still answers her messages within the hour.
- **Rival — Ruairí Flanagan:** A free-trader captain she humiliated on a border run; he spent eight years patiently arranging her ruin and succeeded.
- **Enemy — Hunahpu Tanaka:** The engineering crewmate who sold her ship's access codes to Flanagan and disappeared with the company safe; Hana doesn't know where, but she has the codes Tanaka used and a name that hasn't surfaced on any registry since.

### Backstory
She left an airless rock at eighteen with a name nobody on the rock cared about and learned, over four hard terms, what that name was worth on the trade lanes — enough to open a door, never enough to close one behind her. The wound is Tanaka: not the lost ship, not the lost rank, but the fact that someone she'd trusted with her codes walked off into the dark and she still doesn't know whether they're dead, or rich, or working on Flanagan's next move. Her direction now is simple — she wants a deck under her boots again, a manifest in her hand, and the chance to find one of those two faces across a docking bay.
