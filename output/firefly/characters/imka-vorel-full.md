## **Imka Vorel**
*Muscle — A dying gunhand from the deadliest battlefield of the war, sending platinum home to a sister who thinks she's still healthy.*

|---|---|
| **Role** | Muscle |
| **Homeworld** | Hera (Rim) |

### Attributes
- **Agility** d6 — moves economically, never wastes a step she doesn't have to take
- **Alertness** d10 — clocks every exit, every hand near a holster, every wrong note in a room
- **Intelligence** d6 — practical and literate, reads contracts twice
- **Strength** d8 — still strong enough to end most fights in one exchange
- **Vitality** d4 — lungs scarred to ribbons; a flight of stairs is a negotiation
- **Willpower** d8 — the only thing she's got left in surplus

### Skills
- Alertness/Perception d10

- Unarmed Combat d8

### Distinctions
1. **Watches the Exits** — She's never been ambushed and never will be, but the constant scanning marks her in any room as someone who expects violence — which sometimes invites it.
2. **Doesn't Hit Children or Medics** — A line she'll take a beating to hold; clients who learn it either trust her more or fire her on the spot.
3. **Coughs Blood When She Thinks No One's Looking** — The secret is the wound; the day someone sees it is the day her price drops and her enemies multiply.

### Signature Asset
**Petra's Letters** d6 — A bundle of thirty-one unopened envelopes from her sister on Harvest, kept in an oilcloth wrap inside her vest; she can't read them and can't throw them away, and somehow that contradiction keeps her standing.

### Complications
**Tienjin Lung, Late Stage** d6 — She needs twelve hundred platinum for a treatment course and she's burning through what time she has left taking jobs hard enough to pay for it.

### Equipment
- A heavy pistol and a combat knife, both hands occupied when required
- A medpatch kit — she goes through more than most, and half of them now are for the blood she coughs into her sleeve
- A combat vest worn by default, the inside lining stitched with a hidden pocket for Petra's letters
- A coin from a world she swore she'd never go back to — a pitted Hera ten-credit piece from before unification, carried since she was sixteen; it's the only thing she has from her mother, who died in the first month of the war while Imka was off-world earning passage money that arrived three days too late.

### Backstory
She left Hera at fifteen to crew freighters and was three systems away when the Alliance came for her homeworld and her mother both, and she has spent the twenty years since taking the hardest contracts she could find as though enough bruises might pay down that debt. Two months ago on Persephone she was hired to walk a twelve-year-old courier named Ruo through six blocks of Eavesdown and only five of those blocks went right; she told the boy's family he died clean, and that lie sits heavier in her chest than the Tienjin scarring. Now she's on the *Last Dance* because Captain doesn't ask why she takes the front of every boarding action, and she's calculating whether twelve hundred platinum will arrive before her lungs do — and whether, if it doesn't, she has the courage to finally open one of Petra's letters before the end.
