## **Bohdana "Bo" Pavelić**
*Settlement doctor turned reluctant traveler — patches up bodies with the same patience she uses on a busted still.*

| | |
|---|---|
| **Role** | Doctor (frontier-trained, no formal credentials) |
| **Homeworld** | Constance, Rim |
| **Key Attributes** | Willpower d10 / Intelligence d8 / Strength d8 |

**Distinctions:** Learned Medicine From a Book and a Lot of Mistakes / Doesn't Flinch / Keeps a Ledger of Every Patient

**Demeanor:** Broad-shouldered and unhurried, with calloused hands and a habit of humming while she works. Speaks plain, listens longer than most, and laughs at her own dry jokes a half-beat before anyone else does.

**Wants:** A working centrifuge and three crates of antibiotics — the hill fever outbreak in her valley has outpaced what she can brew herself.

**Secret:** Six years back she let a wounded Alliance officer die on her table when she could've saved him. Told the family it was the bullet. It was the morphine she chose not to give.

**Hook:** She'll trade real doctoring — no questions, no Cortex records — for passage to Persephone and back, plus help carrying "agricultural supplies" through a customs checkpoint that knows her by name.

**Connection:** Her niece **Temperance**, fifteen and sharp as a scalpel, is apprenticing under her. Bo is terrified the girl will grow up to be a better doctor in a worse world, and is quietly saving coin to send her Core-side to a real school — whether Temperance wants to go or not.
