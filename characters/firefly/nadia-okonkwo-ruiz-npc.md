## **Nadia Okonkwo-Ruiz**
*Hired muscle — sharp eyes, slow words, reads a room before she enters it*

| | |
|---|---|
| **Role** | Bodyguard-for-hire, occasional bounty retrieval |
| **Homeworld** | Shadow (Rim) — left before the glassing, never went back |
| **Key Attributes** | Alertness d10 / Agility d8 / Strength d8 |

**Distinctions:** Browncoat who kept the coat / Quicker to listen than to swing / Can't read past a ration label

**Demeanor:** Stands in corners, not doorways. Speaks maybe four sentences a job, but her eyes track every hand in the room — and she remembers faces from a decade back, which is more than most folks want.

**Wants:** A clean three weeks of work to pay a Persephone forger for a set of papers under a name that isn't hers.

**Secret:** She's illiterate — hides it by claiming bad eyesight, has a tiny voice-recorder fob she uses to "take notes." If anyone hands her a contract, she stalls until she can get someone to read it aloud as a "courtesy."

**Hook:** Approaches the crew at a Eavesdown bar with a job — escort a "package" (a teenage girl) off-world. Won't say who's paying. The pay's good. The girl is *not* who Nadia was told she'd be.

**Connection:** **Sergeant Bo Vitek**, her old squad leader from Serenity Valley, now drinks himself stupid on Beaumonde. She sends him coin every month under a false name. If he dies before she sees him sober, she swears she'll burn down the bar that killed him — and she means it literal.
