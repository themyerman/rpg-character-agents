## **Tomás Okonkwo-Veil**
*Freelance gunship navigator — talks like he's reading from a logbook, even when he's threatening you.*

| | |
|---|---|
| **Playbook** | Pilot |
| **Heritage** | Wanderers |
| **Background** | Military |
| **Vice** | Luxury / a private booth at the Saffron Veranda, a floating restaurant in Procyon orbit |

**Key Actions:** Helm (d8) — uncanny at threading debris fields blind; Scan (d8) — reads sensor ghosts the way other people read faces; Command (d8) — gives orders in a calm flat voice that people obey before they decide to.

**Demeanor:** Compact, deliberate, immaculate flight jacket with the insignia carefully removed — you can still see the stitch holes. Smiles only after a successful burn, and only at the instruments.
**Wants:** A clean transponder code and a quiet six-month layover somewhere with real gravity and real bread.
**Secret:** During the Cygnet Withdrawal he was the nav officer who plotted a course that stranded two evac transports — civilians — to save the warship he was on. Command praised him. He resigned the next cycle.
**Hook:** Approaches the crew at a portside bar with a job: a Hegemonic destroyer captain is hosting a banquet on a luxury liner, and Tomás knows the room, the menu, and the exact moment dessert is served. He won't say why he wants the captain humiliated rather than killed.
**Connection:** Idrissa Pell, the cook from his old warship, who keeps a noodle stall on Bellhollow Station. She is the only person who knows what he did, and she still feeds him for free — which he cannot forgive her for.
