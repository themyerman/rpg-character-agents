## **Vaelinor Sennethyr**
*Wood Elf Outcast (Hermit-Exile) — A barefoot elf who hasn't spoken to her own kind in eighty years, and won't say why.*

| | |
|---|---|
| **DEX** | 15 (+2) *(13 +2 racial)* |
| **WIS** | 12 (+1) *(11 +1 racial)* |
| **CHA** | 11 (+0) |

**Demeanor:** She stands too still and stares too long — the patience of something that has watched seasons rot. When she finally speaks, her Elvish is archaic, and her Common is hesitant and oddly formal, like she's translating from a language no one else remembers.
**Wants:** A particular kind of pale mushroom that only grows on the north face of a barrow three days east — she needs it before the next new moon, and she will not say why.
**Secret:** She wasn't exiled. She fled. During the last Feywild incursion she abandoned her younger brother to a hag's bargain to save herself, and her people believe him dead by the hag's hand alone. The hag is still alive, and so, somewhere, is he.
**Hook:** She offers to guide the party through old-growth forest no map shows — in exchange, they help her retrieve "a thing left behind" from a barrow she refuses to enter herself.
**Connection:** Her brother **Aelar**, who she left in the Feywild eight decades ago. She has convinced herself he is dead. She is wrong, and the mushroom is the first step in a ritual she's been quietly assembling for forty years.
