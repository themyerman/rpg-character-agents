## **Korven "Half-Lung" Drask**
*Drifter (Survivor) — forty-two years of bad luck and still standing*

| | |
|---|---|
| **UPP** | 973883 |
| **Career** | Drifter — 6 terms, walked out of a Megacity penal block at 42 |
| **Notable Skills** | Streetwise 2, Melee 2, Survival 1 |

**Demeanor:** Big, quiet, and slow to move — until he isn't. The wheeze when he breathes too hard is the punctured lung from the alley on Thassik Station; he doesn't explain it and doesn't complain about it. Watches doors.

**Wants:** One last job that doesn't end with him in a cell or a gutter — and to find out what the runaway he pulled out of a press-gang twelve years ago actually wants from him.

**Secret:** The Cr105,000 stashed across three starports isn't savings. It's the cut from a job he was supposed to split with two partners who didn't make it out. He's been waiting for someone to come looking for it.

**Hook:** He knows every back corridor, fixer, and pressure point on any dockside world you care to name — and he'll trade that knowledge for passage, for protection, or for the right reason. He's not looking for trouble. Trouble has a way of finding him anyway.

**Connection — the Runaway:** No name given yet, just a message from a station Korven doesn't recognize, saying only: *"I found what you were looking for. Don't come alone."*
